## 🚀 What is CMDR SYS? **CMDR SYS** is your personal mission log, intel tracker, and notepad — built for Elite Dangerous Commanders who need to keep track of the galaxy without tabbing out of the cockpit. Whether you're hunting bounties, plotting trade routes, or tracking faction reputation, CMDR SYS keeps your notes organized and accessible. > *"In a galaxy of 400 billion star systems, you're going to need a notepad."* --- ## ✨ Features | Feature | Description | |---|---| | 📝 **Smart Notes** | Organize your mission intel, system data, and objectives | | 🪐 **System Tracking** | Log visited systems and points of interest | | 📱 **Cross-Platform** | Available on Desktop (Electron) and Android | | 🔄 **v2.0 Data Transfer** | Seamlessly migrate your data between versions | | 🌌 **Elite-themed UI** | Dark, immersive interface inspired by the game's HUD | --- ##
